Technical Highlights

Our autonomous campus taxi system offers seamless, eco-friendly transportation for students across the campus. With a user-friendly online booking platform, students can easily reserve seats and specify their pickup and drop-off locations within the campus vicinity. The system is designed to operate efficiently, providing round-the-clock service, ensuring students have access to convenient transport at all hours. The autonomous vehicles are equipped with advanced navigation systems that allow them to travel safely and smoothly through designated campus routes, offering a stress-free and efficient way to commute.

The system intelligently monitors the state of charge (SOC) of each vehicle's battery. Once the SOC reaches a certain threshold, the vehicle autonomously halts its service and drives itself to the charging station for replenishment. This ensures that the taxis are always available in optimal condition, minimizing downtime and maximizing service reliability. By combining cutting-edge autonomous driving technology with sustainable energy management, our campus taxi system provides a practical, efficient, and environmentally friendly transportation solution tailored to meet the unique needs of students and staff.
