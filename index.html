Project: BEE

Autonoumous swarm of drone (ongoing)

This is my 4th year semester project/thesis work with swarm robotic system using multiple drones for surveillence, 3D mapping and coordinated navigation to ensure autonomous flights and complete specific actions autonomously. We developed and opensourced fully customizable quadcopter firmware on ESP32 platform as a part of the thesis task. The Visual Inertial Odometry pipeline is now being built for a single drone. Once a single unit flight operation is stable enough we can shift to adding more units in the system developing the swarm incrementally.

Project : Automama

Autonoumous Ground vehicle

I worked in the project as an undergraduate research assistant under the supervision and funding of Dr. Shahidur Rahman. In the project I designed the vehicle steering system and feedback sensor frames, tested varios control algorithms based on 3D perception feedback of our custom made stereo vision system. Currently the autonomous navigation runs on local map based on segmentation model with depth perception forming a BEV local map to costmap with potential feilds guiding the trajectory . The main application target was to build an autonomous taxi system for students in the campus that has 24 hour service with automatic solar based charging station.

Project : Orca

Autonoumous Underwater Vehicle

I worked on the this project during our 3rd year as a research assistant under the supervision and funding of Dr. Shahidur Rahman. I designed the hull carrier frame, electronics assembly internal frame and internal system and installed proper enclosure with running BLDC propellers. Finally I custom designed and built the Bouyancy system for depth control of the bot. Our main application was under water survey in the ocean and do sea bed mapping for oceanographic research. Also our project qualified for SAUVC 2025 organized by IEEE Oceaninc Society in hosted by NUS and NTU collaborations in Singapore
